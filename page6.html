DeskStop

System Requirements

 

Need to have either of Windows / Linux / MacOS GUI desktop environment.

DeskStop is a versatile, multi-feature clock, up-time and pomodoro timer application. designed for desktop use across Windows, Linux, and macOS platforms. With a range of customizable features, it is ideal for users who want a personalized, functional time-keeping and productivity tool with visual appeal. The application combines utility and aesthetic customization to enhance user experience.

 

Features:

  • Uptime Tracker: Monitor your system’s uptime to gauge productivity or device longevity.

  • Analog & Digital Clocks: Choose between classic analog or modern digital displays to suit your style.

  • Multiple Time Zones: View and manage time across multiple regions ideal for remote teams and frequent travelers.

  • Pomodoro Timer: Integrated Pomodoro timer to boost productivity with customizable work and break intervals.

  • Pomodoro Sounds: Choose from various sound effects for the Pomodoro timer to suit your work rhythm.

  • Customizable UI: Personalize DeskStop’s look with a range user interface settings like font, Background Color & Opacity to blend with your desktop environment.

  • Various Dial Images: Select from different dial styles for the analog clock, adding a unique touch to your time display.

  • Alarms: Set one-time or recurring alarms with custom tones for reminders, tasks, or events.
